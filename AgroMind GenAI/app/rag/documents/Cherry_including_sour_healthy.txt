Disease Name: Healthy Plant

Crop:
Cherry

Scientific Name:
Prunus avium

Plant Condition:
Healthy (No disease)

Affected Plant Parts:
Leaves, branches, and fruits are healthy

Description:
The cherry tree is healthy and growing normally. The leaves are green and fresh, and the fruits develop properly. Good orchard care helps maintain plant health and fruit production.

Cause:
- No disease detected
- Proper plant care
- Good growing conditions

Favorable Conditions:
- Proper sunlight
- Healthy soil with nutrients
- Regular watering
- Good air circulation

Symptoms:
- Green and fresh leaves
- No powder, spots, or discoloration
- Strong branches
- Healthy fruit development

Plant Condition Spread:
- Not applicable because the plant is healthy

Steps to Keep Plant Healthy:
1. Maintain regular pruning of branches.
2. Apply balanced fertilizer for proper growth.
3. Remove weeds around the tree.
4. Monitor the orchard frequently for early signs of disease.
5. Keep the orchard area clean.

Recommended Nutrients / Fertilizer:
- Organic compost
- NPK balanced fertilizer
- Farmyard manure

How to Apply:
- Apply fertilizer around the root area.
- Water the tree after fertilizer application.
- Follow recommended fertilizer schedule.

Cost per Acre (Approximate):
- Fertilizer cost: ₹1000 – ₹3000 per acre
- Labor cost: ₹300 – ₹700
- Total cost: about ₹1300 – ₹3700 per acre

Plant Monitoring Time:
Check tree condition every 7–10 days.

Prevention:
- Maintain proper pruning
- Use healthy planting material
- Keep orchard clean
- Maintain proper spacing between trees

Best Farming Practices:
- Regular orchard inspection
- Balanced fertilizer use
- Good soil management
- Proper pruning of branches

Effect on Yield:
Healthy trees produce better quality fruits and higher yield.

Farmer Advice:
Continue good orchard practices such as regular pruning, proper fertilization, and monitoring to keep cherry trees healthy.
