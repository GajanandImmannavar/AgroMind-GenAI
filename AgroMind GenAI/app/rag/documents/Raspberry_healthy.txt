Disease Name: Healthy Plant

Crop:
Raspberry

Scientific Name:
Rubus idaeus

Plant Condition:
Healthy (No disease)

Affected Plant Parts:
Leaves, stems, and fruits are healthy

Description:
The raspberry plant is healthy and growing normally. The leaves are green and fresh, and the fruits develop properly. Proper farming care helps maintain plant health and improve fruit production.

Cause:
- No disease detected
- Proper plant care
- Good growing conditions

Favorable Conditions:
- Adequate sunlight
- Healthy soil with nutrients
- Proper irrigation
- Good air circulation

Symptoms:
- Green and healthy leaves
- No unusual spots or discoloration
- Strong stems
- Normal fruit development

Plant Condition Spread:
- Not applicable because the plant is healthy

Steps to Keep Plant Healthy:
1. Maintain proper watering based on plant needs.
2. Apply balanced fertilizer for good growth.
3. Remove weeds around the plants.
4. Monitor plants regularly for early signs of disease or pests.
5. Keep the field clean.

Recommended Nutrients / Fertilizer:
- Organic compost
- NPK balanced fertilizer
- Farmyard manure

How to Apply:
- Apply fertilizer near the root area.
- Water the plants after fertilizer application.
- Follow recommended fertilizer schedule.

Cost per Acre (Approximate):
- Fertilizer cost: ₹1000 – ₹3000 per acre
- Labor cost: ₹300 – ₹700
- Total cost: about ₹1300 – ₹3700 per acre

Plant Monitoring Time:
Check plant condition every 7–10 days.

Prevention:
- Maintain proper watering
- Use healthy planting material
- Keep field clean
- Maintain proper spacing between plants

Best Farming Practices:
- Regular field inspection
- Balanced fertilizer use
- Good soil management
- Proper weed control

Effect on Yield:
Healthy plants produce good quality raspberries and higher yield.

Farmer Advice:
Continue good farming practices such as proper watering, fertilization, and regular monitoring to keep raspberry plants healthy.
