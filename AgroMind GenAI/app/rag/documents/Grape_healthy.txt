Disease Name: Healthy Plant

Crop:
Grape

Scientific Name:
Vitis vinifera

Plant Condition:
Healthy (No disease)

Affected Plant Parts:
Leaves, vines, and fruits are healthy

Description:
The grape plant is healthy and growing normally. The leaves are green and fresh, and the fruits develop properly. Good vineyard management helps maintain plant health and improves grape production.

Cause:
- No disease detected
- Proper plant care
- Good growing conditions

Favorable Conditions:
- Adequate sunlight
- Healthy soil with nutrients
- Proper irrigation
- Good air circulation

Symptoms:
- Green and healthy leaves
- No unusual spots or patches
- Strong vines
- Good fruit development

Plant Condition Spread:
- Not applicable because the plant is healthy

Steps to Keep Plant Healthy:
1. Maintain regular pruning of vines.
2. Apply balanced fertilizers for good growth.
3. Remove weeds around the plants.
4. Monitor the vineyard regularly for early signs of disease or pests.
5. Keep the vineyard clean.

Recommended Nutrients / Fertilizer:
- Organic compost
- NPK balanced fertilizer
- Farmyard manure

How to Apply:
- Apply fertilizer near the root area.
- Water the plant after fertilizer application.
- Follow the recommended fertilizer schedule.

Cost per Acre (Approximate):
- Fertilizer cost: ₹1000 – ₹3000 per acre
- Labor cost: ₹300 – ₹700
- Total cost: about ₹1300 – ₹3700 per acre

Plant Monitoring Time:
Check plant condition every 7–10 days.

Prevention:
- Maintain proper pruning
- Use healthy planting material
- Keep the vineyard clean
- Maintain proper spacing between vines

Best Farming Practices:
- Regular vineyard inspection
- Balanced fertilizer use
- Proper soil management
- Good weed control

Effect on Yield:
Healthy plants produce better quality grapes and higher yield.

Farmer Advice:
Continue good vineyard practices such as regular pruning, proper fertilization, and monitoring to keep grape plants healthy.
