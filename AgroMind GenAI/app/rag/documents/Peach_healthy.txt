Disease Name: Healthy Plant

Crop:
Peach

Scientific Name:
Prunus persica

Plant Condition:
Healthy (No disease)

Affected Plant Parts:
Leaves, branches, and fruits are healthy

Description:
The peach tree is healthy and growing normally. The leaves are green and fresh, and the fruits develop properly. Good orchard care helps maintain plant health and improves fruit production.

Cause:
- No disease detected
- Proper plant care
- Good growing conditions

Favorable Conditions:
- Adequate sunlight
- Healthy soil with nutrients
- Proper irrigation
- Good air circulation

Symptoms:
- Green and healthy leaves
- No unusual spots or patches
- Strong branches
- Good fruit development

Plant Condition Spread:
- Not applicable because the plant is healthy

Steps to Keep Plant Healthy:
1. Maintain regular watering based on plant needs.
2. Apply balanced fertilizers for proper growth.
3. Prune trees properly to maintain plant shape.
4. Remove weeds around the tree.
5. Monitor the orchard regularly for early signs of disease or pests.

Recommended Nutrients / Fertilizer:
- Organic compost
- NPK balanced fertilizer
- Farmyard manure

How to Apply:
- Apply fertilizer around the root area.
- Water the plant after fertilizer application.
- Follow the recommended fertilizer schedule.

Cost per Acre (Approximate):
- Fertilizer cost: ₹1000 – ₹3000 per acre
- Labor cost: ₹300 – ₹700
- Total cost: about ₹1300 – ₹3700 per acre

Plant Monitoring Time:
Check tree condition every 7–10 days.

Prevention:
- Maintain regular watering
- Use healthy planting material
- Prune trees properly
- Keep orchard clean

Best Farming Practices:
- Regular orchard inspection
- Balanced fertilizer use
- Good soil management
- Proper pruning of branches

Effect on Yield:
Healthy trees produce better quality fruits and higher yield.

Farmer Advice:
Continue good orchard practices such as proper watering, fertilization, pruning, and regular monitoring to keep peach trees healthy.
