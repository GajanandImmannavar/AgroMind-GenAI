Disease Name: Healthy Plant

Crop:
Strawberry

Scientific Name:
Fragaria × ananassa

Plant Condition:
Healthy (No disease)

Affected Plant Parts:
Leaves, stems, flowers, and fruits are healthy

Description:
The strawberry plant is healthy and growing normally. The leaves are bright green and fresh, and the fruits develop properly. Good farming care helps maintain plant health and improve fruit production.

Cause:
- No disease detected
- Proper plant care
- Good growing conditions

Favorable Conditions:
- Adequate sunlight
- Healthy soil with nutrients
- Proper irrigation
- Good air circulation

Symptoms:
- Bright green and healthy leaves
- No unusual spots or damage
- Strong plant growth
- Healthy fruit development

Plant Condition Spread:
- Not applicable because the plant is healthy

Steps to Keep Plant Healthy:
1. Maintain regular watering based on plant needs.
2. Apply balanced fertilizer for proper growth.
3. Remove weeds around the plants.
4. Monitor plants frequently for early signs of disease or pests.
5. Keep the field clean.

Recommended Nutrients / Fertilizer:
- Organic compost
- NPK balanced fertilizer
- Farmyard manure

How to Apply:
- Apply fertilizer near the root area.
- Water plants after fertilizer application.
- Follow recommended fertilizer schedule.

Cost per Acre (Approximate):
- Fertilizer cost: ₹1000 – ₹3000 per acre
- Labor cost: ₹300 – ₹700
- Total cost: about ₹1300 – ₹3700 per acre

Plant Monitoring Time:
Check plant condition every 7–10 days.

Prevention:
- Maintain proper watering
- Use healthy planting material
- Keep field clean
- Maintain proper spacing between plants

Best Farming Practices:
- Regular field inspection
- Balanced fertilizer use
- Proper soil management
- Good weed control

Effect on Yield:
Healthy plants produce good quality strawberries and higher yield.

Farmer Advice:
Continue good farming practices such as proper watering, fertilization, and regular monitoring to keep strawberry plants healthy.
